How Linkport Works
What is linkport ?
Linkport is a free chrome extension that lets you import LinkedIn contacts to your CRM. This tool is perfect for sales teams which allows them to populate LinkedIn contact details like name, phone, email, or organization directly into your CRM without leaving LinkedIn, in just one click. With Linkport, you can fill your CRM with an unlimited number of LinkedIn contacts.
What can I do with Linkport?

Linkport is perfect/ideal for anyone who uses LinkedIn for prospecting. With Linkport, you can;

  • Fill your CRM with hot LinkedIn prospects from both regular LinkedIn and the LinkedIn Sales Navigator with just a single click.
  • Quit switching & save time importing contact details between LinkedIn & your CRM.
  • Fill in contact details for the fields you already have in your CRM like the first name, email, phone, organization, etc. in no time.
  • Save about 2+ hours every day and 10+ hours each week from copy-pasting LinkedIn contacts to your CRM.
    • How does Linkport work with my CRM?
  • Add LinkPort Chrome Extension
  • Sign-up to LinkPort with your chosen CRM account
  • Once connected, go to ‘Add to CRM’ next to your LinkedIn contact/lead.
  • Click on the button to automatically fill in details and import contacts to your CRM.
