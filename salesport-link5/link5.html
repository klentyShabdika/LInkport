How to prevent Duplicates on Linkport-Salesforce?
When you import a lead from LinkedIn to Salesforce CRM using Linkport, the “Add to Salesforce” option next to the leads’ name changes to “Added to Salesforce”.

The “Added to Salesforce” doesn’t appear for a lead that you have previously added if you have not mapped the LinkedIn profile to a field in Salesforce.

Here is how you can map the LinkedIn profile to a field in Salesforce CRM,

  1. Go to Salesforce CRM, and click on “Setup”
  2. You will be redirected to the Setup page, go to Contacts → Fields.
  3. If you have the “Salesforce Lightening Experience”, go to the “Settings” page & search for “Object Manager” on the “Quick Finds” search bar.
  4. Look for the “Contacts” object. You will be redirected to the Contact - Details page.
  5. Go to “Fields & Relationships” & click on the “New” button.
  6. To Create the “New Custom Field”, choose the “URL” data type. Click on “Next”
  7. In Step 2, fill the Field Label & Name; click on “Next”.
  8. In step 3, you will be asked to establish field-level security. Choose all the profiles you want to grant access to.
  9. In step 4, add a field to the required page layout & click on “Save” when you are done.
