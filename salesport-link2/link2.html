Salesforce CRM with Linkport

Linkport support docs > Linkport Salesforce > How to import leads from LinkedIn to Salesforce?
Note: Before you start, remember to download the Free LinkedIn to Salesforce Importer extension and connect Linkport to your Salesforce CRM account.
Here’s how you can import leads to Salesforce CRM;
  1. Click on “Go to LinkedIn” on the Linkport Page after choosing the fields you want to map.
    • Note: Find how you can connect Linkport to your Salesforce CRM account here.


  2. Go to your prospect's LinkedIn page & choose the “Add to Salesforce” option.
  3. A linkport plugin will appear. All the required fields will auto-fill. Choose “Add to Salesforce” to add the contact to Salesforce CRM.
