How to change Linkport-Salesforce settings after the setup?
Changing the Linkport-Salesforce settings after the setup

Firstly, when you initially connect Linkport with the Salesforce CRM, you will automatically be redirected to set up your linkport account.

Once you are done with the initial account setup, you are good to go.

To learn about how to set up your Linkport account, click here.
If you have NOT Signed Out of Linkport:
  • You can click on the “Free LinkedIn to Salesforce Importer” extension, which will direct you to connect to Salesforce CRM.
  • Select Your Region & choose the “I agree to Linkport’s Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy” checkbox.
  • Select “Activate with Salesforce” to connect to Salesforce CRM. This will redirect you to the Salesforce CRM “Sign In” page.
  • Once you connect Linkport with Salesforce CRM, you will be directed to the “Set up Your Linkport Account” page.
  • You can now choose to add/edit the required fields that you want to import from LinkedIn.
Change Linkport fields after choosing the Lead in LinkedIn
  • Once you have chosen the lead on LinkedIn, you can click on the “Settings Icons” on the Linkport Plugin.
  • You will be directed to the “Set up Your Linkport Account” page. You can now choose to add/edit the required fields that you want to import from LinkedIn to Salesforce CRM.
