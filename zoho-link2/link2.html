Zoho CRM with Linkport

Linkport support docs > Linkport Zoho > How to import leads from LinkedIn to Zoho?
Note: Before you start, remember to download the Free LinkedIn to Zoho Importer extension and connect Linkport to your Zoho CRM account. Find how you can connect Linkport to your Zoho CRM account here.download the Free LinkedIn to Zoho Importer extension and connect Linkport to your Zoho CRM account here.

Here’s how you can import leads to Zoho CRM;
  1. Click on “Go to LinkedIn” on the Linkport Page after choosing the fields you want to map.


  2. Go to your prospect's LinkedIn page & choose the “Add to Zoho” option.
  3. A linkport plugin will appear. All the required fields will auto-fill. Choose “Add to Zoho” to add the contact to Zoho CRM.

    4. Make sure the field mapping is completed before you import leads from LinkedIn to Zoho.
