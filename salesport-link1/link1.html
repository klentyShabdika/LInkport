How do I connect Linkport to Salesforce?
Where to find the plugin?
You can find it in the chrome webstore here.
To set up your connection,

(Note: Remember to have your Salesforce CRM account logged in)

  • Add the “Free LinkedIn to Salesforce Importer” chrome extension.
  • Connect Linkport to Salesforce by selecting “Activate with Salesforce”
  • Login to your Salesforce Account.
  • You will then be directed to set up your Linkport Account and map the required fields with the Salesforce fields.
  • Go to LinkedIn & add an unlimited number of LinkedIn contacts to your CRM.
