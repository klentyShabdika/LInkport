Mapping Salesforce fields to Linkport

As soon as you connect Linkport to Salesforce CRM, you will be redirected to the “Setup Your Linkport Account” page. You’ll see,
  • Choose display fields
  • Map Linkedin fields with Salesforce fields
  • Auto-lookup contacts with Salesforce
  1. Under the display fields section, the Lead - Last Name & Account - Name will be automatically mapped & you will not be able to edit these. Map any or all of the fields that you would like to be displayed in the plugin when you import the contact from LinkedIn to Salesforce CRM.
  2. In the “Map LinkedIn fields with Salesforce fields” section, the Leads - Last Name & Account - Name are automatically mapped and imported to Salesforce CRM.

    3. Customized mapping:

    • Click on the dropdown next to the corresponding Salesforce field and you will find the option to customize your LinkedIn fields.
    • Once the field is successfully updated, you should be able to map the Salesforce field to the corresponding LinkedIn field.
    • All the other fields can be “mapped” or “not mapped” to the fields while importing leads from LinkedIn to Salesforce CRM.

      • Note: You cannot map two Salesforce fields to the same LinkedIn fields. Even if the settings are updated to the same LinkedIn fields, it will not show up while you import the leads from LinkedIn.

  1. Under the Auto-lookup contact with Salesforce section, you can choose the sections in LinkedIn to automatically lookup contacts with Salesforce.
